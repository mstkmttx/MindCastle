Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

Welcome to the Mind Castle Privacy Policy. Your privacy is critically important to us.

Information We Collect

Mind Castle is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. The app operates primarily locally on your device:

  • Voice Recordings: While using the microphone feature, your voice is processed locally on your device for transcription. Voice recordings are not sent to our servers.
  • Thought Data: All thoughts, reflections, and categories you create are stored only on your device using local storage.
  • Usage Data: Basic, anonymous usage metrics may be collected to help us improve the app's performance and features.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored in Mind Castle is used solely for the purpose of providing the app's functionality:

  • To display your thoughts and reflections within the app
  • To categorize and organize your content
  • To provide personalized insights based on your thoughts
  • To improve the app's performance and features

Data Storage

Mind Castle stores all your thought data locally on your device using the browser's localStorage mechanism. This means:

  • We do not store your thoughts or recordings on our servers
  • Your data remains private and accessible only to you
  • Your data will be lost if you clear your browser data or uninstall the app

In future versions, we may offer optional cloud backup features with appropriate security measures.

Third-Party Services

Mind Castle currently does not utilize any third-party services that would access your thought content.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data in Mind Castle:

  • You can view all data stored in the app
  • You can delete individual thoughts or clear all data
  • You can export your data (feature coming soon)

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
